Lieutenant Anara was a Bajoran engineer assigned aboard Deep Space 9 in 2369. 
She assisted Chief Miles O'Brien in repairing the station systems and subsequently in dealing with a living software program from the Gamma Quadrant, nicknamed "Pup", that had infiltrated the station computers. 
Her part in overloading the system involved ordering the computer to "access musical files in the Bajor master databanks and create a concert program of Bajoran serenas". (DS9: "The Forsaken")
