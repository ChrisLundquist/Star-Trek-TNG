Avesta was a male Bajoran of the 24th century Bajoran Republic. In 2371, he was the governor of the Bajoran colony Prophet's Landing. 
When Kira and Odo visited the colony in 2371 to review security procedures, he invited them both for dinner at his house, but Kira refused, without asking Odo first. This later caused some strain between the two, as Odo felt disrespected, despite the fact that he didn't eat, hated official functions, and would have declined to attend the event if he had been given the opportunity. (DS9: "Heart of Stone")
