Bek was a prylar of the Bajoran religion during the Occupation of Bajor. A vague acquaintance of Odo, the constable of Terok Nor, although the two were not well-associated, the Changeling thought he was a good man. He also knew Kubus Oak, who also resided on the station. During the time of the Kendra Valley Massacre he was the liaison for the Vedek Assembly and the Occupational Government. Two days after the massacre, Bek hanged himself on the Promenade of Terok Nor and claimed in his suicide note that he gave the Cardassians the location of the resistance fighters in the Kendra Valley, which led to him being branded a collaborator.
In actuality, the information was provided by Kai Opaka, who had sought to protect the lives of the citizens in the Kendra Valley, and Bek hanged himself to protect the truth about Kai Opaka's involvement. (DS9: "The Collaborator")
