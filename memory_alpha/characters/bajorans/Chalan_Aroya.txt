Chalan Aroya was a Bajoran woman who owned the Celestial Cafe on Deep Space 9. She often watched Odo walk by her business on his patrol at precisely 0937.
In 2372, she visited Garak's Clothiers to obtain a new dress which she charged to her account. Elim Garak, hoping to set her up with Odo, called the security officer to his shop. Aroya told him that she had noticed him and invited the constable to stop by the cafe on his off hours. After she left, Garak called Odo's letting her go without making a date a crime. Despite being unable to understand humanoids' obsession with mating rituals, Odo did concede that Aroya was lovely.

Several days later, after Odo had been transformed into a humanoid by the Founders, Chalan visited him in Garak's again. She told him how sorry she was to hear of what had happened, adding that despite the challenges of adjusting, there were advantages to being humanoid. (DS9: "Broken Link")
