General Hazar was a flag officer in the Bajoran Militia. 
In 2370, General Hazar ordered Militia forces to not allow any Skrreean vessels to land on the surface of Bajor. Deep Space 9 personnel contacted Hazar when the Skrreean boy Tumak attempted to run the blockade. After being informed that the pilot was only a child, Hazar ordered his forces to stand down, but it was already too late; the damaged Skrreean ship exploded after the radiation it was leaking was ignited by phaser fire, and Hazar could only extend his condolences to Commander Benjamin Sisko. (DS9: "Sanctuary")
