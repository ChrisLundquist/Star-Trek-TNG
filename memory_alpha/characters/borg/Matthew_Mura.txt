Lieutenant Matthew Arliss Mura was a Bajoran 25th century Starfleet operations division officer.


== Early life ==
Mura was born sometime after 2376. (PIC: "Võx")
He had a son. (PIC: "Surrender")


== Starfleet career ==


=== Tactical officer of the USS Titan-A ===
In 2401, Lt. Mura served as the tactical officer aboard the USS Titan-A. He was on bridge duty when First officer Commander Hansen brought guests Admiral Jean-Luc Picard and Captain William T. Riker with her and after the Titan-A departed spacedock on their mission. (PIC: "The Next Generation")


=== Tactical officer of the USS Enterprise-G ===
In 2402, Mura was assigned to newly rechristened USS Enterprise-G as tactical officer. He was present on the bridge of the Enterprise when Ensign Crusher reported for duty and the Enterprise headed out on their shakedown cruise.  (PIC: "The Last Generation")
