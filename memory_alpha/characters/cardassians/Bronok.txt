Bronok was a Cardassian who joined the Cardassian Rebellion in 2375. Rusot considered him to be "experienced" and one of the best soldiers under his command.
He was involved in the planting of a bomb in a Jem'Hadar fighter's intercooler matrix. He and his four companions ignored the orders of Kira Nerys, who had told them to plant it in the secondary plasma relay. Fortunately, the Jem'Hadar didn't discover the bomb, and the ship was destroyed. Nevertheless, Kira and Garak were disappointed that the team had ignored their training and instructions. This led to a heated argument between her and Rusot.
When Rusot protested that the five were some of his best soldiers and mentioned that Bronok was one of the most experienced, Garak and Kira reminded him not to mention their names in case someone was captured. (DS9: "Tacking Into the Wind")
