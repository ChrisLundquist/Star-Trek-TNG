Gul Cazanjian was the regional prefect for a region of Cardassian space. 
In the 2360s, this gul reported an attack on an orbiting monitoring station at Matiovsky IV to Cardassian Central Command. The Kohn-Ma, a Bajoran splinter group, were identified by the gul as the most probable candidate for the party responsible for the attack. (DS9: "Past Prologue")
