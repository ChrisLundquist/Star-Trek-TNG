Glinn Corak was an officer in the Cardassian military during the late 24th century who served aboard the warship Reklar.
In 2369, he served as an aide to Gul Lemec, where he accompanied Lemec to the USS Enterprise-D in an attempt to defuse tensions between the Federation and Cardassia. Actually, the Cardassians were planning a surprise attack on Minos Korva. Their plot was foiled by Captain Edward Jellico who was able to plant mines on the hulls of the Cardassian ships involved. (TNG: "Chain Of Command, Part I", "Chain Of Command, Part II")
