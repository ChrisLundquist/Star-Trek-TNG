Glinn Delgado was a Cardassian military officer in the 2360s. 
He reported suspicions to Cardassian Central Command that the collision of the transport ship Barkano with an asteroid was possibly the work of the Bajoran splinter group Kohn-Ma. In the report, he stated that the computer charts of the region where the Barkano had its accident were altered so as not to show the location of asteroids.
An intelligence report with Delgado's name referenced was stored in the Bajoran Intelligence net, which was accessed in 2369 by Major Kira Nerys when she was looking into Kohn-Ma activities. (DS9: "Past Prologue")
