Entek was a male Cardassian and an operative of the Obsidian Order in the 2370s.
In 2371, he was part of the intelligence operation to expose Legate Tekeny Ghemor's ties to the Cardassian Underground. He captured Major Kira Nerys and altered her to appear to be a Cardassian, Ghemor's daughter Iliana. He then proceeded to pretend to conduct her debriefing from her long-term mission in her father's presence. When Kira continued to resist the story that she was Iliana, Entek became more forceful, giving Ghemor the incentive to help her evade using the Underground.
Entek was successful in exposing Ghemor; however, when Garak, Sisko and Odo arrived to rescue Kira, a fight broke out. Seemingly after it had ended and the group was departing, Entek made the mistake of drawing his weapon on them. Garak was quicker, however, and vaporized Entek, quipping afterwards to his surprised colleagues that it was "a pity. I rather liked him." (DS9: "Second Skin")
