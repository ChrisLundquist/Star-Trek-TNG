Professor Illa Dax was a Cardassian-Trill hybrid who was the host of the Dax symbiont in the late 32nd century. She taught Confronting the Unexplainable, an advanced seminar at the newly recommissioned Starfleet Academy in San Francisco.
In 3195, Dax was approached by SAM, who had been ordered by her Makers to join her seminar. Dax was reluctant as it was already midterms; when SAM proposed to make the mystery of Benjamin Sisko's disappearance her midterm project, Dax replied that if she managed to solve it, she could teach the class herself. Dax later checked in with SAM and told her that the mystery could not be solved with facts alone, using the debate over whether tomatoes belonged in gumbo as a metaphor.
SAM eventually returned to Dax in defeat, despondent in realizing that emissaries like Sisko could not live the lives they wanted, whether they succeeded or failed. Seeing that SAM had learned much, Dax gave her a bound book of Jake Sisko's Anslem, which he had never published. Upon opening it, SAM received a vision of Jake, who helped reconcile her to her own role as an emissary. Dax then revealed her full identity to SAM, and remarked that Sisko would have liked her as, "He loved people who got into trouble for the right reasons." (SA: "Series Acclimation Mil")
