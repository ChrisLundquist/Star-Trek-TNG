Raymond Boone was the alias of a Cardassian agent who assumed the appearance of the Human Starfleet officer Raymond Boone, the real Boone having died in a detention center in 2362.


== History ==
In 2362, this surgically-altered Cardassian infilitrated Starfleet. Shortly after he was discharged from Starfleet for failing several crew performance reviews. "Boone" settled with the officer's family on Volan III and operated a ladarium mining sluice. Boone's wife felt something was different about her "husband", but believed it was due to the torture the Cardassians subjected him to. "Boone" would later leave her that same year.
In 2370, "Boone" was tasked as part of a large operation that was designed at discrediting the Federation and to make it appear that they were sanctioning the Maquis. That same year, the Cardassian traveled to Deep Space 9 where he met O'Brien and recorded his voice. He used O'Brien's recorded voice to break into a weapons locker and thus framed O'Brien for the crime. After analyzing the logs, Commander Sisko traveled to Volan III and apprehended "Boone". It was thought that "Boone" was a Maquis conspirator, until an unidentified Maquis operative contacted Julian Bashir, claiming that "Boone" was not one of them, and that they had nothing to do with the theft.
After examination it was discovered that his first molar had been extracted (as all Cardassian children at 10 years old were required to do) and that his DNA was Cardassian. Sisko then brought him to O'Brien's trial on Cardassia Prime. As soon as the Chief Archon, Makbar, saw "Boone", recognizing the unspoken threat from Sisko that attempting to convict O'Brien would result in him publicly exposing the plan, embarrassing the Cardassian Central Command in front of all Cardassia, as well as potentially triggering a new conflict with the Federation, she ended the trial and O'Brien was turned over to Sisko. (DS9: "Tribunal")
