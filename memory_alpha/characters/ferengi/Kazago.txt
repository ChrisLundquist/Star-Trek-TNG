Kazago was the first officer aboard a Ferengi D'Kora-class marauder commanded by DaiMon Bok in 2364. 
He was involved in the plan to return the USS Stargazer to Starfleet, unaware that Bok was not planning to sell it but to use it to avenge the death of Bok's son who was killed in a battle with the Stargazer in 2355, by gaining revenge on Federation captain Jean-Luc Picard of the USS Enterprise-D.
After connecting, first officer to first officer, with Commander William T. Riker, Riker was able to convince Kazago that Bok was pursing an unprofitable venture. Once Riker proved this to be the case, by presenting one of Bok's thought makers, Kazago relieved Bok from command and placed him under guard. (TNG: "The Battle")
