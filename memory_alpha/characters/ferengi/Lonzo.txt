Lonzo was a male Ferengi, a known tongo hustler on whom Odo received a bulletin in 2371.
The Changeling, who was at the time "joined" with Curzon Dax via the zhian'tara, pointed Lonzo out to Jadzia Dax and mentioned the bulletin, suggesting they could have fun cleaning the Ferengi out. He then preceded to do exactly that, apologizing to Lonzo as they parted. (DS9: "Facets")
