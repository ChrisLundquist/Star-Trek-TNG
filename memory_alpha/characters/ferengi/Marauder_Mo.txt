Marauder Mo was a 24th century Ferengi action figure for male Ferengi children. One of the accessories available for the figures was an energy whip.
As a child, Quark collected several Marauder Mo figures, which were later considered valuable collectibles after he reached adulthood. However, Ishka noted that their value would have been much higher if Quark had left the toys in their original packaging. (DS9: "Ferengi Love Songs")
