DaiMon Nandi was a female Ferengi who lived during the late 24th century. She owned and operated the D'Kora-class vessel known as the Damsel.
Prior to 2383 she was a mentor to Dal R'El. However, she eventually sold him to The Diviner and left him in the chimerium mines on Tars Lamora. She then purchased the robot Pik-Pox with the resulting profit, a partner who Nandi thought was more adept at stealing. (PRO: "First Con-tact")
She later contacted the REV-12 to claim a reward for the location of the USS Protostar from the Diviner. (PRO: "Time Amok")
