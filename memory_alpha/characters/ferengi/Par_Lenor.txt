Par Lenor was a male Ferengi who lived during the 24th century.
In 2368, he claimed to be a member of the Ferengi Trade Mission when he boarded the USS Enterprise-D for the purpose of acquiring Kamala, an empathic metamorph. Lenor and Qol destroyed their own shuttle so that the Enterprise-D would agree to transport them. 
Once on board, Lenor attempted to bribe Ambassador Briam with 20,000 coins of Ludugial gold, but was rejected. When Briam threatened to inform Captain Picard of their true intentions, Lenor and Qol attempted to detain him and he was injured, but subsequently recovered. Lenor and Qol were transported to Starbase 117 shortly thereafter, where they were expected to face charges. (TNG: "The Perfect Mate")
