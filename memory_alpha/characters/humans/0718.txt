Lieutenant 0718 was a sciences division Starfleet officer. He was serving on the bridge of the USS Enterprise in 2259.
0718 was present during the ship's mission to Nibiru. He was also present when the Enterprise was tasked with finding and apprehending Khan Noonien Singh.
Following the Vengeance's attack on the Enterprise, he reported that the latter had sustained major hull damage.
Later, he counted down James T. Kirk and Khan's distance from the Vengeance in meters as they approached the Dreadnought-class starship in thruster suits.
Finally, at the orders of Commander Spock, he gave the order to evacuate the Enterprise as it began its descent into Earth's atmosphere.
In 2260, he was still stationed aboard the Enterprise as they embarked on their five-year mission. (Star Trek Into Darkness)
