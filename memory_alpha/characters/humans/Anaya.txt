Ensign Anaya was a Human Starfleet officer who served as the conn officer aboard the USS Enterprise-D during the mid-2360s.
In 2367, Anaya manned the conn station when the Enterprise-D worked on repairs of the Argus Array and was unable to control the ship when Reginald Barclay under the influence of an alien probe piloted the ship into a self-created subspace distortion. Anaya regained control back when the ship met the Cytherians in the middle of the galaxy. (TNG: "The Nth Degree")
