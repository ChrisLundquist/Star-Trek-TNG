Ensign Angie was a female Human Starfleet command division junior officer who lived during the 2370s, when she served aboard the USS Quito. 
She was a close friend to Beckett Mariner at the time of her death aboard the Quito sometime in the 2370s. Angie was killed when her boyfriend, Niko, was exposed as a Harvongian and attempted to eat her. (LD: "Cupid's Errant Arrow")
