Anna was a Human female. 
She was the sole survivor of a Terellian cargo freighter which crashed on an unnamed class M planet. For seven years, she lived alone on the planet until there was another crash. The survivor of that crash was a Human male, whom she nursed back to health. They fell in love. Several years before 2370, the Iyaarans discovered the remains of the freighter. Within the ship, they found Anna's logs, from which they learned of concepts alien to their culture – love, antagonism, and pleasure. 
Voval later took the form of another Anna in an attempt to recreate and experience that the original Anna had experienced. (TNG: "Liaisons")
