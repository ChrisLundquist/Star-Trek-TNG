Ensign Anya was a Human female Starfleet command division junior officer who lived during the late 24th century. During the early 2380s, she served aboard the USS Cerritos and was friends with Jennifer Sh'reyan, Castro, and Wendy. 
In 2381, Anya attended the salon held in Castro's quarters, where she officially met Jennifer's girlfriend Beckett Mariner. During the salon she performed the interpretive dance she entitled "The Kobayashi Maroon". (LD: "Hear All, Trust Nothing")
