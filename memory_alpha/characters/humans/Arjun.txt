Commander Arjun was a male Sikh Human operations division Starfleet officer who served during the late 24th century. 
In 2380, he served aboard the USS Cerritos and was in the shuttlebay when D'Vana Tendi arrived in the Yosemite, where he chatted with Merp while standing near the Death Valley. (LD: "Second Contact")
The following year, he participated in the training drills presented by Shari yn Yem, where he participated in and failed the simulation, Tribble Infestation. (LD: "I, Excretus")
He was seen sitting in the bar with Dahae when Beckett Mariner sat nearby and told Jack Ransom, Shaxs, and Andy Billups about Captain Carol Freeman's promotion to a new ship. (LD: "First First Contact")
Later, he was in a corridor when Brad Boimler ran past as he attempted to avoid being hunted by K'ranch. (LD: "The Least Dangerous Game")
He was later in the Cerritos' bar chatting with an operations commander from the USS Merced, during the celebration following the California-class' combined defeat of the rogue Texas-class USS Aledo, when T'Lyn entered. (LD: "The Stars At Night")
