Ensign Bandee was a Starfleet nurse serving aboard space station Deep Space 9.
In 2374, Julian Bashir told Miles O'Brien to see Bandee if he needed anything to take the pain from his dislocated shoulder. (DS9: "Inquisition")
She was on duty on stardate 52645.7, when Julian Bashir was incapacitated. Bashir had used a neural interface to link his and Chief Miles O'Brien's minds to the mind of a dying Luther Sloan. Bandee watched over all three men in sickbay, apprising Captain Benjamin Sisko of their conditions, until Bashir was able to break his and O'Brien's link with Sloan and learned that he still was caught in Sloan's mind. (DS9: "Extreme Measures")
A few weeks later, Keiko O'Brien made an appointment with Bandee for Kirayoshi to have a checkup. (DS9: "What You Leave Behind")
