Captain Bandra was a female Human who served as a Starfleet officer in the 32nd century.
In 3189, she attended a meeting at Federation Headquarters with several other starship captains, including L'Teis Kardashev, Rahma, and Saru. Fleet Admiral Charles Vance tasked her and the USS Le Guin with a two-month supply run to Na'Seth. (DIS: "Scavengers")
In 3190, Bandra attended a briefing at Federation Headquarters regarding a massive gravitational anomaly that struck Kwejian and Deep Space Repair Beta Six. (DIS: "Anomaly (DIS)")
