Bardoli was a male Human Starfleet officer who lived during the mid-23rd century. He served in the operations division aboard the USS Enterprise under Captain James T. Kirk in 2268, where he served as one of the ship's security officers.
That year, Bardoli, together with Swenson, was part of a landing party led by Captain Kirk that was searching for the dikironium cloud creature on Argus X. (TOS: "Obsession")
