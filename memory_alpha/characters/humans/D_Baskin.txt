D. Baskin was a Human Starfleet officer in the 22nd century.
In 2154, he was listed as a Flight Software officer on the dedication plaque of the Columbia NX-02. (ENT: "Affliction", dedication plaque)
In January 2155, he was listed as an Environmental Control officer on directory placards at Starfleet Command's Fleet Operations Center, Sol Sector. His office was located in room #4230. (ENT: "Demons", directories)
