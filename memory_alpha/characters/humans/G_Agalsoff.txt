G. Agalsoff was a Human Starfleet officer in the 22nd century.
In 2151, he was listed as an Imaging and Sensor Technologies officer on the dedication plaque aboard Enterprise NX-01. (ENT: "Broken Bow", dedication plaque)
In 2154, he was listed as a member of the Flight Test Crew on the dedication plaque aboard Columbia NX-02. (ENT: "Affliction", dedication plaque)
In January 2155, he was listed as PDRS officer on directory placards at Starfleet Command's Fleet Operations Center, Sol Sector. His office was located in room #3479. (ENT: "Demons", directories)
