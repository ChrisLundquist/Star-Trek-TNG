Johann Sebastian Bach was a 18th century German composer. Among his works was the Cello Suite No. 1 in G major, Concerto for Two Violins, and the Third Brandenburg Concerto. (TNG: "A Matter Of Time", "Lessons"; Star Trek: Insurrection; PIC: "Absolute Candor")
In 2368 Berlinghoff Rasmussen mentioned Bach to Geordi La Forge as a historical figure who was blind. (TNG: "A Matter Of Time")
