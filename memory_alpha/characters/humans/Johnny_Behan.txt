Johnny Behan was a 19th century Human law enforcement officer who served as a county sheriff in the territory of Arizona during the opening of the American frontier during the age of the Ancient West.  By the 1880s, he resided in Tombstone, Arizona at the time of the shootout at the OK Corral on October 26, 1881. Behan was a friend of Ike Clanton and promised him amnesty in case he killed the Earps.
In 2268, Behan was part of a bizarre inaccurate recreation created by the Melkot to execute Captain James T. Kirk and his landing party.  The Melkotian replica of Behan was referred to as the "Tombstone Sheriff" and was closely allied with the Clantons, on a first name basis with Ike Clanton, and had a peculiar sense of humor.  When Captain Kirk, in the role Ike Clanton, approached Sheriff Behan to stop the fight with the Earps, Behan flat out refused, saying he was counting on the Clantons to "get rid of the Earps for him".
