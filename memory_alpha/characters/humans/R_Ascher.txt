R. Ascher was a businessman who worked for the Big Creek Manufacturing and Sales Co. of Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania during the late 1950s. His office was on the first floor.
While on Earth in 1957, the Vulcan T'Mir sold him the rights to a new kind of hook-and-loop fasteners that would later be known as Velcro. She used the money to help pay for Jack's education. (ENT: "Carbon Creek")
