Yeoman Tonia Barrows was a female Human Starfleet enlisted crewman who lived during the mid-23rd century. She served in the operations division aboard the USS Enterprise under Captain James T. Kirk, during a historic five-year mission that starship undertook.
In 2267, Barrows was one of several landing party members briefly trapped on the surface of the Shore Leave Planet. While there, she was accosted twice by recreations of legendary rake Don Juan. She found a dress, exactly the sort she imagined a princess of the "blood royale" might wear.
Barrows was later romanced by Doctor McCoy. She subsequently saw McCoy apparently murdered by the Black Knight, and briefly blamed herself for this, before Kirk helped her regain her composure.
When McCoy returned to the surface, from where he'd been taken for medical care, she took umbrage to a pair of Rigelian chorus girls he brought with him. His apologetic "After all, I am on shore leave" brought him a frosty "And so am I." (TOS: "Shore Leave")
