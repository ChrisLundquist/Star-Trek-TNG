General Chu'vok was a high-ranking Klingon flag officer in the Klingon Defense Forces during the mid-2370s.
Chu'vok participated in Chancellor Gowron's war plans briefing in 2375, where he was to play an instrumental role in Gowron's proposed battle plan against Sarpedion V. In Gowron's strategy, General Martok was to lead fifteen Vor'cha-class battle cruisers into battle to soften Sarpedion's defenses, followed by Chu'vok's forces. (DS9: "Tacking Into the Wind")
