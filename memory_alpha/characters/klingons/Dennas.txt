Dennas was a female Klingon who lived during the mid-23rd century. 
Dennas represented the House of D'Ghor on the Klingon High Council in 2256. When T'Kuvma rallied the twenty-four Great Houses by using the Beacon of Kahless, her vessel answered the call.  While hearing T'Kuvma lay out his desire to unite the Klingon people, Dennas was keen to hear more, and asked how this could be done. (DIS: "Battle at the Binary Stars")

During the first months of the Federation-Klingon War, the House of D'Ghor was nominally independent. In December 2256, they and the House of Mo'Kai secretly arranged a meeting with Vulcan to discuss the possibility of uniting against Kol's forces. They were to meet Ambassador Sarek on Cancri IV. However, the meeting was in fact a ruse by Kol to capture a high-ranking Federation official. Because Sarek was unable to attend due to having suffered from a terrorist attack, Admiral Katrina Cornwell attended the meeting instead, and was captured by Dennas and Ujilli. (DIS: "Lethe")
Dennas was later present in the Klingon High Council Chamber when L'Rell declared her ascendance to the role of Chancellor in 2257. (DIS: "Will You Take My Hand?")
