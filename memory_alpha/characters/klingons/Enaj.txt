Enaj was a Klingon who served on the Oversight Council of the Klingon Defense Force in the late 24th century, alongside Bargh and K'orin.
In 2382, Enaj confirmed to Bargh that Ma'ah had the right to initiate the Ritual of J'ethurgh to regain his captaincy, as mentioned by Brad Boimler. She later joined with K'orin in voting to allow Ma'ah to transfer to another fleet not under Bargh's command, and in witnessing Ma'ah's invocation of the Rite of Forced Conscription on Bargh. (LD: "A Farewell To Farms")
