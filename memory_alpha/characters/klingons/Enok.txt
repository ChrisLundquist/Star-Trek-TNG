Enok was a Klingon who lived in the 32nd century. A member of the House of Kraag, he was married to Drekol and L'vanna, with whom he had two sons, Thar and Jay-Den. He was a player of the Klingon guitar.
Like most of the Klingon diaspora, Enok and his family were itinerant refugees. In 3194, they were encamped in the wilderness of Krios Prime. One day at a trading post, Thar was stabbed by a trader with a poisoned dagger while attempting to acquire a forbidden Federation dermal regenerator for Jay-Den. Enok and Drekol managed to drag him back to their camp before he perished. Jay-Den and Drekol subsequently fell out over Jay-Den's intent to join Starfleet Academy rather than become a warrior. Afterward, Enok and his spouses departed the planet in their ship, leaving Jay-Den behind.
In 3195, Enok, Drekol, and L'vanna were among those headed for a refugee camp on Taurus VII when their heavy cargo carrier suffered a critical mechanical failure and crashed. The three were found alive and brought to the new Klingon homeworld of Faan Alpha. (SA: "Vox In Excelso")
