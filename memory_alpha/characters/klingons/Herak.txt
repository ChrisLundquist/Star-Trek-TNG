Herak was a Klingon warrior who lived in the 23rd century.
In 2261, Herak was one of two subordinates who accompanied Bytha to Kenfori, on her quest to restore honor to the House of Ra'Ul by killing Doctor Joseph M'Benga. Inside an abandoned Federation agricultural research facility, the Klingons captured M'Benga and Captain Christopher Pike, only to be set upon by "zombies" created by the chimera agent. Herak survived this first encounter, but on the second was bitten by one of the zombies, which infected him with the agent. Bytha promptly vaporized him with her disruptor. (SNW: "Shuttle to Kenfori")
