Huss was a Klingon warrior, the daughter of A'trom. 
In 2373, she was inducted into the Order of the Bat'leth, in an emotional ceremony presided over by Klingon chancellor Gowron at Ty'Gokor. It was at that same event that the individual believed to be General Martok was revealed to be a Changeling spy masquerading as the general. (DS9: "Apocalypse Rising")
