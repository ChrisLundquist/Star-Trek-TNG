Kee'Bhor was a Klingon medical officer in the Klingon Empire in the 24th century. In the 2360s, Kee'Bhor served aboard the IKS Toh'Kaht, including the starship's 2369 bio-survey mission to the Gamma Quadrant.
Kee'Bhor became convinced that the recently retrieved Saltah'na energy spheres were causing the crew's increasingly erratic behavior. Kee'Bhor tried to warn first officer Hon-Tihl, but, under the influence of the spheres, he killed Kee'Bhor suspecting a plot by his captain. (DS9: "Dramatis Personae")
