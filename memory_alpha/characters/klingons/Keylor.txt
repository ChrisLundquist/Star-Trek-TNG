Key'lor was a Klingon warrior who lived during the late 24th century, where she served as a lower decker aboard of the Klingon Bird-of-Prey IKS Che'Ta'. 
In 2381, Key'lor was "tasked with scraping out the worm barrels and replenishing their blood supply;" considered by some to be one of the worst jobs on the ship. (LD: "wej Duj")
She was later on the bridge when the Che'Ta' was captured by the Nova One. (LD: "Twovix") She later followed G'reck after he took the ship over from Ma'ah. (LD: "The Inner Fight")
