Almak was a sub-commander in the Romulan Star Empire in the late 24th century.
In 2374, he was in command of the Romulan D'deridex-class warbird T'Met. Accompanied by two more Romulan warbirds, Almak contacted the USS Prometheus requesting an identification as he was able to understand the person he was talking to was not Commander Rekar.
In fact, he was talking to The Doctor who tried to hide the fact that he was not a Romulan. Almak's request to activate the viewscreen was not answered so he opened fire. (VOY: "Message in a Bottle")
