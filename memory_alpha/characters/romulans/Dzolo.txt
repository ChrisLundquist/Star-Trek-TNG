Dzolo was a Romulan who served in Starfleet during the late 32nd century.
In 3195, Dzolo was among the first incoming class of War College cadets to attend at the newly recommissioned Starfleet Academy campus in San Francisco. She was the captain of the Mugatos, the College's calica team, and was friends with B'Avi and Kyle Djokovic. (SA: "Beta Test", "Vitus Reflux")
Dzolo and B'Avi pranked the Starfleet Academy cadets by beaming them, in varying states of undress, from the locker room to random public locations. With Commander Zeeren Kelrec's aid, they also released a mocking parody of an Academy recruitment video using footage from their calica tryouts. She then participated in an unsanctioned calica match between the Mugatos and the Academy team, the Laplings. Dzolo and her comrades were later themselves victims of a prank by the Academy cadets, in which they used Vitus reflux to force the evacuation of the War College dormitories. (SA: "Vitus Reflux")
By midterms, Dzolo and B'Avi were engaged in a campus "turf war" with Darem Reymi. She was further dismayed to find him and a group of Academy cadets at The Academy nightclub, which they considered War College territory. As the night wore on, a bar fight broke out between the two sides over Djokovic dancing with Jay-Den Kraag, and Dzolo was punched by an intoxicated SAM. (SA: "Series Acclimation Mil")
