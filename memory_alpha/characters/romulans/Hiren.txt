Hiren was the praetor of the Romulan Star Empire in the late 2370s. 
In 2379, Praetor Hiren rejected Commander Suran's proposal to join forces with Shinzon, and ordered the Reman insurgents to be suppressed by force. Shortly after, he and the Romulan Senate were assassinated by a thalaron radiation device planted by Senator Tal'aura. (Star Trek Nemesis)
