Doctor Kabath was a female Romulan physician who worked for the Borg Reclamation Project on the Artifact in the late 24th century.
In 2399, Kabath removed several Borg implants from Patient 8923/3, a former Borg drone, while being observed by Soji Asha and Narek. Soji objected to Kabath referring to the patient as "Nameless", to which Kabath replied that "Nameless" was a name. (PIC: "Maps and Legends")
Later that year, Kabath informed Narissa that there was no medical reason why Ramdha had not regained consciousness. (PIC: "Broken Pieces")
