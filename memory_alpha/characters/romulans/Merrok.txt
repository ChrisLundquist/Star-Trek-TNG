Merrok was a proconsul of the Romulan Senate during the 24th century. Elim Garak claimed to have met him once, when he was working as a gardener at the Cardassian embassy on Romulus. A few weeks later, Merrok was poisoned, one of many "unexpected" deaths within the Romulan government that year (including a Romulan Ambassador).
Years later, Odo speculated that the Romulans assassinated Merrok, and that Garak was responsible for assassinating Sub-Commander Ustard. Garak said he was "getting warmer". (DS9: "Broken Link")
