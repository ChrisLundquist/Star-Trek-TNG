Pel was a female Romulan who lived on Freecloud in the 24th century. She was the wife of Gabriel Hwang.
In 2399, Pel and Gabriel were expecting their first child, a daughter. She had an appointment at Stardust City Reproductive Health Services when Gabriel was visited by his mother Raffi Musiker. Pel greeted Raffi briefly before Gabriel hustled them both away. (PIC: "Stardust City Rag")
By 2401, her child had been born. (PIC: "The Next Generation")
