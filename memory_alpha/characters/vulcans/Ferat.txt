Fer'at was a Vulcan psychiatric analyst stationed at the Vulcan Compound on Earth. 
In 2153, he posed as a pathologist checking Jonathan Archer for pyritic radiation exposure in an attempt to determine Archer's mental stability, as the Vulcans placed very little credence in Archer's claim to have talked to the Cabal's master from the future. 
While he was performing the examinations, however, Phlox scanned the Vulcan database and discovered Fer'at's actual specialty. Confronting the analyst, Phlox asked him to get out of the sickbay. Archer ordered him off Enterprise NX-01 and had Phlox escort him to the airlock. (ENT: "The Expanse")
