Lieutenant Kinis, a Vulcan junior officer at Starfleet Academy, was the role played by a member of Species 8472 in training program.
He took part in the training aboard Terrasphere 8 created by Species 8472 in the Delta Quadrant in 2375. They were preparing for a possible invasion into Federation space. The Kinis re-creation served as an aide to the re-creation of Admiral Bullock and handed PADD's with assignments to the re-creations of Larsen, Young, and Reiskin. (VOY: "In the Flesh")
