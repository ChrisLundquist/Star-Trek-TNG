Subaltern Lorot was a member of the Vulcan High Command who was assimilated by the Borg.
In 2375, when the USS Voyager encountered a Borg vinculum, Seven of Nine began to experience the personalities of various assimilated individuals, including Lorot.
As Lorot, Seven suggested that a wounded security officer should logically be taken to the infirmary. Noting that "clearly he was attacked," "Lorot" suggested Tuvok use caution.  
Later, when Tuvok mind melded with Seven to stabilize her neural pattern, "Lorot" complained, "I fail to see the logic in this meld, Commander." (VOY: "Infinite Regress")
