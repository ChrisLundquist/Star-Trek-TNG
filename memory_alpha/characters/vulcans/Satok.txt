Minister Satok was a Vulcan government official and head of the V'Shar in 2370. 
That year, he informed Commander William T. Riker that Tallera was, in fact, not a V'Shar operative. Satok later took Tallera into custody and began a search for the remainder of the Vulcan Isolationist Movement. He assured Jean-Luc Picard that all three pieces of the Stone of Gol would be destroyed. (TNG: "Gambit, Part II")
