Shara was a female Vulcan lower decker who served aboard the Vulcan cruiser VCF Sh'vhal. 
When nearing the end of her second duty shift in late 2381, she was asked by T'Gai if she was interested in a game of chess. Shara declined, as she planned on "composing an arrangement for the lute, which should take several hours." (LD: "wej Duj")
