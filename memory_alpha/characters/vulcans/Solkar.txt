Solkar of Vulcan was a male Vulcan of the 21st century and 22nd century. He was the father of Skon, grandfather of Sarek, and great-grandfather of Sybok and Spock. (ENT: "The Catwalk"; Star Trek III: The Search for Spock)
Solkar was the first Vulcan ambassador to Earth and, as such, was the answer to a crossword puzzle clue which a female crewmember was trying to solve while forced to live in Enterprise NX-01's catwalk for several days. Captain Archer supplied her with the answer. (ENT: "The Catwalk")
Solkar's grandson Sarek followed in his father's footsteps and became an ambassador, as did great-grandson Spock, after his service in Starfleet. (TOS: "Journey to Babel"; TNG: "Unification I")
In the early 25th century, the Federation starship USS Solkar was named in his honor. (PIC: "The Last Generation")
