40 Eridani A I was the first planet orbiting the star 40 Eridani A in the Vulcan system. This planet was located in the Vulcan sector, Beta Quadrant, and was approximately 16 light years from Sol.
This uninhabited class B planet had no moons.
In 2259, the planet's name, its position in the system, and its orbital path were seen on a display graphic of the shuttle Cervantes. (SNW: "Charades")
