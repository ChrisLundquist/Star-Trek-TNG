Aaamazzara was an inhabited planet in the Epsilon Serpentis system. Located in the Alpha Quadrant, this planet was the homeworld of the Aaamazzarites and was approximately 72 light years from Sol.


== History ==
In the 24th century, Commander Jerry Goldsmith, a crewmember aboard the USS Enterprise-D, was born on this non-Federation planet. In 2369, his planet of origin was listed on the chart "USS Enterprise Personnel Database", which was displayed on a PADD accessed by Captain Jean-Luc Picard. (TNG: "The Chase", okudagram)
In the 32nd century, Aaamazzara was a Federation member planet. It was listed with other members on a video wall in the USS Athena. (SA: "Kids These Days")


== Stellar cartography ==
In the 3191, this planet's special designation as a homeworld was noted on a star chart. (DIS: "Red Directive")
In 3195, this planet's position was labeled on a Federation star chart which was displayed in Starfleet Academy's Stellar Cartography laboratory. Planets affiliated with the Federation, including this one, had blue labels. (SA: "Beta Test")
