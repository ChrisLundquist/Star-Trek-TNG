Adarak Prime was the prime planet in its system. Located in the Alpha Quadrant, this planet was in Cardassian space.


== History ==
In 2375, during the Dominion War, Adarak Prime was the location for a weapons depot, which was defended by a single Cardassian garrison. This depot was considered by Odo and Garak as a vulnerable facility that the Cardassian Liberation Front could target for attack. They identified a weakness in the depot's perimeter defenses which could be exploited by a small team. Garak would disable the depot's security protocols. The rebels were against raiding the planet and killing their own countrymen. Kira Nerys replied by stating that during the Occupation of Bajor, she didn't want to attack any facility populated by Bajorans, but she did because they were collaborators. She also said that the Dominion would place Cardassians at every single one of their bases once they learned the rebels would not attack their own people. (DS9: "When It Rains...")


== Stellar cartography ==
In 3196, this planet's position was labeled on a Federation star chart that displayed the placement of Omega-47 mines by the Venari Ral around the center of the Federation in the Alpha and Beta Quadrants. This planet was located outside this region. (SA: "300th Night")
