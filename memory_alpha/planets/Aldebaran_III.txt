Aldebaran III was the inhabited third planet in the Aldebaran system. The planet was located in the Beta Quadrant and was approximately 67 light years from Sol. (TOS: "The Deadly Years"; TNG: "Up The Long Ladder", okudagram)
During the 2260s, Doctors Janet and Theodore Wallace performed experiments using various carbohydrate compounds to slow down the degeneration of plant life at the Federation outpost on this planet. (TOS: "The Deadly Years")
In the 32nd century, Aldebaran III was a Federation member planet. It was listed with other members on a video wall in the USS Athena. (SA: "Kids These Days")
