Aldebaron  III was the inhabited third planet of its star system. This planet was located somewhere in the Alpha or Beta Quadrant. This Federation planet was the home to the Aldebaron Colony and a Starfleet installation.
In 2265, the USS Enterprise made a stopover at this planet to pick up Dr. Elizabeth Dehner from the Aldebaron colony. (TOS: "Where No Man Has Gone Before")
In 2371, the Ferengi Belongo, nephew of Grand Nagus Zek, was detained on Aldebaron III by the local Starfleet authorities. (DS9: "Past Tense, Part I")
