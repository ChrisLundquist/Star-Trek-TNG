Alera was a planet visited by crewmembers from the USS Dauntless in 2384. This planet was located somewhere in the Alpha or Beta Quadrant.
In that year, there were two teams on the planet. The first team, consisting of Aaron Cavit, Tuvok, and Harry Kim, were conducting an analysis of the planet. The second team, consisting of Alexander Honigsberg, Ashmore, and Tabor, were gathering resources on the planet. Megan Delaney, in a shuttle piloted by Ahni Jetal , was on her way to the planet to join up with the other crewmembers. (PRO: "Supernova, Part 1")
