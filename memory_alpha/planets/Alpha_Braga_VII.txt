Alpha Braga VII was the inhabited seventh planet in its star system.  This planet was located somewhere in the Alpha or Beta Quadrant.
In 2328, it was the destination for passengers traveling from New Paris Colony Starbase via commercial transport. Wendy Neuss had traveled on the SS Manoa and Jay Chattaway and Eddie Williams had traveled on the SS Wisconsin. (TNG-R: "Inheritance", okudagram)
