Alpha Carinae V was the inhabited fifth planet in the Canopus system. The planet was located in the Beta Quadrant and was approximately 309 light years from Sol.
This planet was the homeworld of the non-corporeal Drella. (TOS: "Wolf in the Fold")
Lieutenant Jonathan West, a crewmember aboard the USS Enterprise-D, was born on this non-Federation planet. In 2369, his planet of origin was listed on the chart "USS Enterprise Personnel Database", which was seen on Jean-Luc Picard's PADD. (TNG: "The Chase", okudagram)
