Alpha Eridani II was the inhabited second planet of the Achernar system. The planet was located in the Beta Quadrant and was approximately 139 light years from Sol. This planet was aligned directly between Earth and Argelius II.
In the 22nd century, an Earth colony was founded on the planet.
In 2156, the Redjac entity was responsible for ten women being knifed to death in the settlement of Heliopolis on Alpha Eridani II. (TOS: "Wolf in the Fold")
