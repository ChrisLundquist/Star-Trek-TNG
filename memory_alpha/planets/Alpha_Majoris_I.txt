Alpha Majoris I was the inhabited first planet in the Alpha Majoris system. The planet was located in the Kiley Local sector, Alpha Quadrant, and was approximately 123 light years from Sol.
This was the homeworld of the cloud creature known as the mellitus. (TOS: "Wolf in the Fold")
By 2259, the Federation had established first contact with this planet. This civilization had developed warp travel and had or was in the process of being inducted into the Federation by this year. (SNW: "Strange New Worlds")
In 2328, both the SS Kogin and the SS Wisconsin transported Max Gabl and Craig Galloway, respectively, to this planet from Dytallix B. (TNG-R: "Inheritance", okudagram)
