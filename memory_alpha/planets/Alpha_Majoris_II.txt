Alpha Majoris II was the second planet in the Alpha Majoris star system. The planet was located in the Kiley Local sector, Alpha Quadrant, and was approximately 123 light years from Sol.
In 2259, the planet's position in the system and its orbital path were seen on a display graphic. Its orbital path took it close to Alpha Majoris I and past Alpha Majoris VI. (SNW: "Strange New Worlds")
