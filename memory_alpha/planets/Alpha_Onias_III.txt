Alpha Onias III was the uninhabited third planet of the Alpha Onias system. This planet was located near the Federation-Romulan border in the Onias sector, Beta Quadrant. 
Although it was class M, Starfleet survey teams listed Alpha Onias III as barren and inhospitable with an atmosphere containing high, but non-toxic, levels of volcanic gases, including sulfur dioxide, methane, and hydrogen sulfide. 
Barash, a humanoid child, was hidden in a cavern by his mother after their homeworld was invaded in the mid-24th century. The cavern was equipped with neural scanners that were able to transform matter into any form imagined, so that Barash could live in safety. He later left the planet for the USS Enterprise-D in 2367.
