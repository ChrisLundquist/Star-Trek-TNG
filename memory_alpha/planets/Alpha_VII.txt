Alpha VII was the inhabited seventh planet in its star system. This planet was located somewhere in the Alpha or Beta Quadrant.
In 2328, this was the destination of Marion Cordry. She had traveled from the New Paris Colony Starbase on the SS Kogin. (TNG-R: "Inheritance", okudagram)
In Brad Boimler's 2381 holonovel Crisis Point II: Paradoxus, Alpha VII was one of the worlds labeled on the star map tattooed on Illustor's loose skin. The "HA" in the name formed part of the word Ki-ty-ha when the skin flaps were pushed together. (LD: "Crisis Point 2: Paradoxus")
