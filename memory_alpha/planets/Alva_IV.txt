Alva IV was the fourth planet in its star system. This planet was located somewhere in the Alpha or Beta Quadrant.
The planet's surface featured both forests and oceans. By 2382, one such forest was the site of a derelict Orion medical frigate from the time of the Great Plague over three hundred years prior. D'Vana Tendi and her band of Orion pirates fought with a group of Blue Orions for the right to salvage the frigate, with the former succeeding and piloting the ship off the surface.
