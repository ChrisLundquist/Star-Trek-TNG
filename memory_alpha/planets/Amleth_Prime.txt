Amleth Prime was the inhabited prime Class M planet in the Amleth system. Located in a sector of the Alpha Quadrant, this planet's system was surrounded by an emission nebula which happened to be in Cardassian space. The planet was in the same spatial region as Rakal and the Loval system.
This nebula rendered cloaking devices ineffective, making the station on this Cardassian planet more difficult to attack in 2372. (DS9: "Return to Grace")
