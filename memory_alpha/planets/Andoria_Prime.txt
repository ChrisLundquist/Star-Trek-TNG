In the alternate reality, Andoria Prime was the inhabited prime planet in its star system. This planet was located somewhere in the Alpha or Beta Quadrant.
In 2259, a 7 News presenter reported on a status update for unrest on this planet. The name of this planet was seen on a headline in the report, which was in a collection of graphics and video media displayed on a powerwall in the offices of Christopher Pike and Alexander Marcus at Starfleet Headquarters. (Star Trek Into Darkness)
