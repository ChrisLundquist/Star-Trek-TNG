Angel I was the inhabited first planet of the Angel system. The planet was located in the Beta Quadrant and was approximately 101 light years from Sol. This remote planet was located close to the Federation-Romulan border and was of strategic importance to the United Federation of Planets.
Angel I was a M-class planet and the homeworld of a humanoid species. The planet supported carbon-based flora and fauna and was sparsely populated with intelligent life. The planet had no platinum deposits. (TNG: "Angel One")
