Ariannus was an inhabited planet that was located somewhere in the Alpha or Beta Quadrant. This planet was located in a region of the galaxy that was governed by the Federation in the 23rd century.


== History ==
Ariannus was established as a vital transfer point for the regular space commercial lanes prior to stardate 5730. At that time, there were a billion people living on the planet. The nearest starbase was Starbase 4.
In 2268, Ariannus was infected by a bacterial invasion that threatened to destroy all life on the planet. The crew of the USS Enterprise, working in tandem with the planet's ministry of health, conducted an orbital decontamination mission to save the planet's inhabitants. (TOS: "Let That Be Your Last Battlefield")
In the 32nd century, Ariannus was a Federation member planet. It was listed with other members on a video wall in the USS Athena. (SA: "Kids These Days")


== Stellar cartography ==

In 2293, the location of Ariannus in the Milky Way Galaxy was labeled in the star chart "The Explored Galaxy", which was on display in James T. Kirk's quarters aboard the USS Enterprise-A. (Star Trek VI: The Undiscovered Country, okudagram)
In 2365, the location of Ariannus was labeled in a Subspace Comm Net Ops star chart, which was seen in the courtroom on Starbase 173. This planet was connected to Janus VI and Kling by subspace lines. (TNG: "The Measure Of A Man")
