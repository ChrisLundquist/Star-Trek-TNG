Planet 10 (DIM-8) was a planet that was located somewhere in the Alpha or Beta Quadrant. This planet was near the Ficus sector. 
In 2137, the SS Buckaroo Banzai was launched from Earth on a mission to Planet 10 (DIM-8). It was listed on the chart "Interstellar Expeditions to Sectors 184/02-185/38: Launched From Sol System, Earth Dates 2123-2190", which was seen on a viewer in Captain Jean-Luc Picard's ready room aboard the USS Enterprise-D.
