The Arcos was an Antares-class Federation freighter that was in service during the mid-24th century. Tan Tsu served aboard the Arcos in the 2360s.
In 2367, the Arcos was destroyed by a warp core breach over Turkana IV. The ship's two-man crew survived in an escape pod, but landed on the war-torn planet where they were captured by the Alliance. They were later rescued by an away team from the USS Enterprise-D. (TNG: "Legacy")
