=== NCIA-93 types ===

Two unnamed NCIA-93 type vessels helped NCIA-93 to search for Michael Burnham and Spock's shuttle. (DIS: "Light and Shadows")
Several more ships of this type were part of the Section 31 armada that was utilized by Control at the Battle near Xahea with one of the ships having the registry NI-1101. (DIS: "Such Sweet Sorrow", DIS: "Such Sweet Sorrow, Part 2")


=== Classified two-nacelled starship ===

Under the command of the rogue Section 31 AI Control, they were among the ships that intercepted the USS Discovery and the USS Enterprise near Xahea. These ships were marked as classified in the Federation database. These ships shared a similar design profile to that of later Miranda-class starships. (DIS: "Such Sweet Sorrow", DIS: "Such Sweet Sorrow, Part 2")


=== Classified four-nacelled starship ===

At least three vessels of this type served as part of Section 31's fleet in the mid-23rd century. Under the command of the rogue Section 31 AI Control, they were among the ships that intercepted the USS Discovery and the USS Enterprise near Xahea. These ships were marked as classified in the Federation database. 
During the Battle near Xahea, three of these ships were destroyed by being rammed by a Klingon cleave ship. (DIS: "Such Sweet Sorrow", DIS: "Such Sweet Sorrow, Part 2") 


=== Drone ship ===

A number of Section 31 starships could deploy drone ships, a type of armored auxiliary space vessel that could be stacked onto their hulls. (DIS: "Such Sweet Sorrow, Part 2")
