The USS Ahwahnee (NCC-2048) was a 23rd century Federation Constitution II-class starship operated by Starfleet.
In 2278, the Ahwahnee appeared on a Starship Mission Assignments chart on the bridge of the USS Bozeman after exiting a temporal causality loop in 2368. (TNG: "Cause And Effect")
In 2293, the Ahwahnee was assigned a deep space exploration mission in Sector 21835. The ship was named on a Starship Mission Assignments chart on the USS Enterprise-A bridge.
Later that year, the Ahwahnee was named as one of the ships on station in Sectors 21166-8 in the "Operation Retrieve" command briefing. (Star Trek VI: The Undiscovered Country)
