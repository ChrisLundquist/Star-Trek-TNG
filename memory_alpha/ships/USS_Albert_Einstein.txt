The USS Albert Einstein (NCC-65123) was a 24th century Federation starship operated by Starfleet. 
In 2364, Captain Layne Friedman was the commander of this ship. He received an order about patrol assignments from Admiral Gregory Quinn of Starfleet Operational Support Services. Later that year, this order was read by Lieutenant Commander Data during an investigation into an alleged conspiracy in the highest levels of Starfleet Command. (TNG: "Conspiracy" okudagram)
