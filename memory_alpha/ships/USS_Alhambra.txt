The USS Alhambra (NCC-75256) was a Federation California-class starship operated by Starfleet in the late 24th century. 
In 2380, Doctor T'Ana accidentally boarded this ship instead of the USS Cerritos, while both ships were on a layover at a Federation spacedock. Due to a number of odd discrepancies, T'Ana believed she was in a parallel dimension before being informed of her error. (LD: "Veritas")
In 2381, the Alhambra joined an armada of California-class ships to defend their sister ship, the Cerritos, from the USS Aledo. After successfully destroying the Aledo, the armada went to Douglas Station. (LD: "The Stars At Night")
