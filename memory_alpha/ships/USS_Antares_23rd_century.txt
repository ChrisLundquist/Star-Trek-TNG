The USS Antares was a Federation starship operated by Starfleet in the 23rd century.
Christopher Pike and Una Chin-Riley had served aboard the Antares prior to their time on the USS Enterprise. (DIS: "Brother"; SNW: "Memento Mori")
In 2259, Chin-Riley wore a Starfleet Remembrance Day pin commemorating the crewmates she had lost from the Antares. (SNW: "Memento Mori")
