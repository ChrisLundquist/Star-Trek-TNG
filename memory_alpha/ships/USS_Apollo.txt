The USS Apollo (NCC-50585) was a 24th century Federation starship operated by Starfleet. 
In 2365, Captain Dennis McCarthy was the commanding officer of this ship. In that year, the Apollo was assigned a deep space exploration mission in Sector 532. This starship was listed in the Starship Deploy Status chart that was on display in the courtroom of Starbase 173. (TNG: "The Measure Of A Man", okudagram)
