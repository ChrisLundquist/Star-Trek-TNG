The USS Archer (NCC-76725) was a Ross-class Federation starship in service to Starfleet during the early 25th century.
In 2401, the Archer joined a Starfleet armada coordinated by the Singularity to deflect a massive energy burst from a newly forming transwarp conduit. (PIC: "Farewell")

Later that year, the Archer participated in Frontier Day celebrations near Sol Station. Along with the rest of the fleet, it was seized by the Borg Collective after its younger crew were assimilated by a Borg signal transmitted via Jack Crusher. (PIC: "Vox")
