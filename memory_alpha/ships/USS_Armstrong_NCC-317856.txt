The USS Armstrong (NCC-317856) was a Federation Constitution-class starship operated by Starfleet during the 32nd century. 
When the USS Discovery arrived at Federation Headquarters in 3189, Armstrong was one of the nearby starships maintaining the station's protection field. (DIS: "Die Trying")
In 3190, under the command of Captain Imahara, the Armstrong rescued the occupants of UFP-11 after the shuttle crashed on Kokytos. (DIS: "All Is Possible")
