USS Artemis (NCC-50532) was a Federation starship that was in service with Starfleet in the mid-24th century. 
In 2365, Captain Peter Lauritson was the commanding officer of this ship. In that year, this ship was repaired at Starbase 74. The ship was listed on the Starship Deploy Status chart that was on display in the courtroom of Starbase 173. (TNG: "The Measure Of A Man" okudagram)
