The USS Atlantis (NCC-52716) was a 24th century Federation starship operated by Starfleet.
In 2365, Dick Brownfield was the commanding officer of the Atlantis. In that year, the ship was assigned a diplomatic mission to Alderaan. The starship was named on a Starship Deploy Status chart that was on display at the courtroom of Starbase 173. (TNG: "The Measure Of A Man", okudagram)
