The USS Bellerophon (NCC-62048) was a 24th century Federation Nebula-class starship operated by Starfleet.
In 2366, the Bellerophon participated in the battle against the Borg at Wolf 359. It engaged the attacking Borg cube, alongside the starship USS Yamaguchi, while the cube had tractored the USS Saratoga. It was ultimately destroyed in the battle along with thirty-eight other starships. (DS9: "Emissary")
In 2384, the Bellerophon appeared on the bridge chart of the USS Dauntless logistics strategic data display. (PRO: "Supernova, Part 1")
