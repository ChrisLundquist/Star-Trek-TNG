The USS Bellerophon (NCC-74705) was a 24th century Federation Intrepid-class starship operated by Starfleet. 
Under the command of Admiral William Ross, the Bellerophon transported Federation delegates to Romulus for a conference on Dominion War issues in 2375. (DS9: "Inter Arma Enim Silent Leges")
