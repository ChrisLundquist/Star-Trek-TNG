The USS Billings was a 24th century Federation starship operated by Starfleet.
Kathryn Janeway served on the Billings as a commander. During her first year, Janeway sent an away mission to survey a volcanic moon. The shuttle was damaged by a magma eruption, and three of the Billings' crew were severely injured. The next day, out of remorse, Janeway returned to the moon to complete the mission herself. Tuvok was also on the ship during this period, and observed the action. 
He mentioned the incident to Chakotay in 2375 when the commander asked if he had ever seen Janeway exhibit a guilt complex before. (VOY: "Night")
