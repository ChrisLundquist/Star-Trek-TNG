The USS Bonestell (NCC-31600) was an Oberth-class Federation starship that was in service to Starfleet during the latter half of the 24th century. 
In 2366, the Bonestell participated in the Battle of Wolf 359, where it was destroyed by the Borg, along with 38 other starships. 
Just as Benjamin Sisko entered his quarters on board the USS Saratoga, during the evacuation, the Bonestell was hit by a cutting beam pulse from the Borg cube. The cutting beam destroyed the front saucer section of the ship in a massive explosion, sending the derelict hulk hurtling past the Saratoga. (DS9: "Emissary")
