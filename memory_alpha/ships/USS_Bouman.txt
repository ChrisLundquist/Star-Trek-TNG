The USS Bouman was a 23rd century Federation starship operated by Starfleet. It was the assignment of Cadet Thira Sidhu's husband.
The Bouman was used in a fictional scenario designed to test Sidhu. In this scenario, it sent out a distress call which was picked up by the Enterprise, which was in the same sector. When the Enterprise approached, it appeared dead in the water, but an admiral aboard the ship signaled that the Tholians had taken the ship. The admiral ordered the Enterprise to steer clear, but Captain Pike instead rescued the admiral, and then resisted her when she tried to abort the rescue mission. Two hours later, Pike found himself at Starbase 28, arrested for mutiny. (ST: "Ask Not")
