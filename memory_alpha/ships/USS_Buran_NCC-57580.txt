The USS Buran (NCC-57580) was a 24th century Federation Challenger-class starship operated by Starfleet. 
In 2366, the Buran fought the Borg at the Battle of Wolf 359. It was destroyed, along with thirty-eight other starships. (TNG: "The Best of Both Worlds, Part II") 
By 2368, the Buran's wreckage had been towed to the Surplus Depot Z15 located in orbit of Qualor II. (TNG: "Unification I")
