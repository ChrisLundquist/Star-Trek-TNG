USS Bushwacker (NCC-52518) was a Federation starship that in service with Starfleet in the mid-24th century. 
In 2365, Captain Adele Simmons was the commanding officer of this ship. In that year, this ship was assigned a mission of  planetary exploration in Sector 542. This starship was listed in the Starship Deploy Status chart that was on display in the courtroom of Starbase 173. (TNG: "The Measure Of A Man", okudagram)
