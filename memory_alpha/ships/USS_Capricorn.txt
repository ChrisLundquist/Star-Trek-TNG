The USS Capricorn was a Constitution-class Federation starship operated by Starfleet in the 32nd century.
In 3195, the Capricorn, along with the USS Crimson, USS Horizon, USS Lexington, and USS Riker, joined the USS Athena above Faan Alpha, where they fought a mock battle against a Klingon fleet that allowed the Klingon diaspora to claim the planet from the Federation as spoils of war. (SA: "Vox In Excelso")
