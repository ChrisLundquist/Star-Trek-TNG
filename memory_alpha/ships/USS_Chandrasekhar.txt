The USS Chandrasekhar (NCC-91816) was a Sutherland-class Federation starship in service to Starfleet during the early 25th century.
In 2401, the Chandrasekhar was inside Sol Station when travel pod 05, carrying Captain William T. Riker and Admiral Jean-Luc Picard, was on route to the USS Titan. (PIC: "The Next Generation")
