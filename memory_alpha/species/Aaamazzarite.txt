The Aaamazzarites were a humanoid species from the planet Aaamazzara, in the Epsilon Serpentis system, in the Alpha Quadrant. with whom the Federation had made first contact by the late 23rd century.
Two representatives visited Starfleet Headquarters air tram station in San Francisco on stardate 7410.2. (Star Trek: The Motion Picture; PIC: "Disengage"; DIS: "Red Directive")
