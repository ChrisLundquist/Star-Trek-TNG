The Aamaarazan were a humanoid species who were known to wear breathing masks. 
In 2286, the Aamaarazan were serving in the United Federation of Planets, where two members of the Federation Council attended the trial of James T. Kirk. (Star Trek IV: The Voyage Home) 
In 2293, another duo represented their Federation interests at the Khitomer Conference. (Star Trek VI: The Undiscovered Country)
In 3189, they were present on the planets Hima and Hunhau. (DIS: "That Hope Is You, Part 1", "Scavengers")
In 3190, at the Multilateral DMA Strategy Assembly, the Aamaarazan representative voted against diplomacy with Species 10-C. (DIS: "...But to Connect")
