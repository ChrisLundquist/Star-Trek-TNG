The Akoszonam were a humanoid species who have been associated with the United Federation of Planets since at least the late 24th century. 
"Squiddled" was an Akoszonam expression meaning "destroyed beyond repair". (DIS: "Kobayashi Maru")


== History ==
During the 2380s, there were Akoszonam living on Earth and Mars. (ST: "Children of Mars")
After the Burn in the mid-31st century, Akoszonam were present in both Starfleet and the Emerald Chain. (DIS: "There Is A Tide...", "Kobayashi Maru")


== Biology ==
Distinguishing features included a faint cranial ridge that converged at the bridge of their nose, several patches of papules along their cheeks and brows, with rows of the same on their hands, and bright blue blood. Their tongue was also much longer than that of an average Human, and some members possessed striking pale blue eyes. (ST: "Children of Mars")
