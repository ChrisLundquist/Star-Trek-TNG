The Alpherati were a spacefaring species native to the planet Alpherat. They were known for being brilliant military strategists, with a commensurately impressive training academy, as well as for never reaching out to other races first. They used fire eels for protection and brought them everywhere, even to planets with unsuitable environments.
A noted Alpherati tradition was the Kometa, a highly ritualized dinner of Kometa fish and soup, which was held on diplomatic occasions.
In 3195, Chancellor Amal of the Alpherati training academy contacted Commander Zeeren Kelrec of Starfleet Academy about a possible collaboration between their institutions. Amal traveled to Earth, where Kelrec hosted a Kometa in her honor. (SA: "Series Acclimation Mil")
