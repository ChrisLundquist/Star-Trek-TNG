The Ayt were a humanoid species known to visit Earth in the alternate reality. Ayt were humanoid and possessed feather-like structures on their [[head]s]. They also could connect their tongues together when they greeted each other. Instead of fingernails, they had claws.
In 2259, two Ayt met in the Port of San Francisco bar and connected their tongues. (Star Trek Into Darkness)
