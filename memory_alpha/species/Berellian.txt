The Berellians were a species known to Starfleet by 2368.
Christopher Hobson claimed that no one would think of making a Berellian an engineer, just as no one would think of making a Klingon a counselor. With these examples he wanted to prove his point – that an android like Data would not make a good captain – when Data was given temporary command of the USS Sutherland. (TNG: "Redemption II")
