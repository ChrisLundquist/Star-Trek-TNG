The Brenari were a humanoid species who inhabited an area of space near the Devore Imperium in the Delta Quadrant. They were persecuted by the Devore because of their telepathic abilities.
In 2375, the USS Voyager smuggled twelve Brenari survivors from a stricken freighter through Devore space by suspending them in the transporter during Devore inspections. The refugees eventually escaped Devore space through a wormhole aboard two of Voyager's Class 2 shuttles. (VOY: "Counterpoint")
By 2384, at least one Brenari was seen serving in Starfleet aboard the USS Dauntless. As a child, this Brenari was one of the twelve that were aboard Voyager. While guarding now-Vice Admiral Kathryn Janeway in the brig, the Brenari officer brought up Janeway and Voyager saving her people and thanked her for it. Janeway's previous kindness caused the Brenari to believe her about the threat that Starfleet was facing and release her from the brig. (PRO: "Mindwalk", "Supernova, Part 1")
