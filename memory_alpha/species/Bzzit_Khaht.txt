The Bzzit Khaht were a sentient species, whose homeworld was a member of the United Federation of Planets by the late 23rd century. 
In 2286, a Bzzit Khaht councilor attended a Federation Council trial of James T. Kirk. (Star Trek IV: The Voyage Home)
In 2381, a framed photograph of a Bzzit Khaht patron holding a bottle was displayed on the wall of a bar on Starbase 25. (LD: "An Embarrassment Of Dooplers")
