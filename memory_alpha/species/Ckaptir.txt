The Ckaptirs were a humanoid species.


== History ==
In the early 24th century, a Ckaptir was among the visitors to the Baraam space station. (Star Trek: Section 31)
According to a holographic re-creation of the events, Ckaptirs served in Starfleet before the Burn, by the time Kaminar joined the United Federation of Planets. (DIS: "Su'Kal")
In 3188, a Ckaptir trader was present at the Mercantile marketplace in Requiem, Hima. (DIS: "That Hope Is You, Part 1")
After the Burn, by 3189, many members of the species served as Starfleet officers. (DIS: "Die Trying", "Scavengers", "There Is A Tide..."). In 3190, a Ckaptir cadet joined the newly reopened Starfleet Academy. (DIS: "Kobayashi Maru")
Later that year, Ckaptir representatives were present at the Multilateral DMA Strategy Assembly and voted against diplomacy with Species 10-C. (DIS: "...But to Connect") The next year they participated in the Millennium Celebration at Federation Headquarters. (DIS: "Red Directive")
In 3191, a Ckaptir archivist worked at the Eternal Gallery and Archive. (DIS: "Labyrinths")


== Biology ==

Ckaptirs had three tendrils on each side of their heads and two pairs of barbels below their mouths. Size depending on the individual can range from much shorter than or similar to average Human. (DIS: "Kobayashi Maru", "...But to Connect")
