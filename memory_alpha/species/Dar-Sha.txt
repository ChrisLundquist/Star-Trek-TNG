The Dar-Sha were a humanoid species.
Starfleet Academy Cadet Genesis Lythe was part Dar-Sha, from her father's side of her family, and was virtually identical to humans except for thin ridges above her eyes instead of eyebrows. (SA: "Kids These Days")
