The Deakohns were a humanoid species.


== Biology ==
The Deakohns had a reptilian- or amphibian-like appearance. They had large heads with four nostrils and round protrusions on each side of the head. 
They had brown or gray skin with black or dark brown spots, while the head protrusions could be blue or orange in color. Their eye colors included black and orange. (DIS: "...But to Connect")
Some members of the species grew facial hair. (SA: "Beta Test")


== History ==
In the early 24th century, a Deakohn was among the visitors to the Baraam space station. (Star Trek: Section 31)
After the Burn, in the 32nd century, various members of the species served as Starfleet officers. (DIS: "Under the Twin Moons"). In 3190, Deakohn cadets joined the newly reopened Starfleet Academy. (DIS: "Kobayashi Maru")
in 3190, Deakohns liquidated a large quantity of latinum trying to solve the problem of getting a ship inside the Dark Matter Anomaly. They were later among the Federation member worlds and allies present at the Multilateral DMA Strategy Assembly and voted for diplomacy with Unknown Species 10-C. (DIS: "...But to Connect")
