The Debrune were an ancient offshoot of the Romulans.
In the 4th century, the Debrune had an outpost on the planet Barradas III. In 2370, the ruins left by the Debrune on Barradas III were raided by a group of mercenaries led by Arctus Baran during their search for the lost fragments of the Stone of Gol. (TNG: "Gambit, Part I")
