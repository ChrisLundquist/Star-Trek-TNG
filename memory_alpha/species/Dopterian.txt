The Dopterians were an often unscrupulous humanoid species found throughout the Alpha Quadrant. The species' homeworld was located in the Dopteria system.
They were distant relatives of the Ferengi, with which they shared an inability to be read by telepathic species such as Betazoids. (DS9: "The Forsaken"; TNG: "Firstborn")
The Dopterians also employed a type of automated attack weapon. (DS9: "Business as Usual")
