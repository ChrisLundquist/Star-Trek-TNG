The Douwd were a non-corporeal species self-described as "immortal beings of disguises and false surroundings". They were able to take on physical forms if they wished, and could make themselves appear Human. They had the ability to create illusions that seemed real to sensors, transporters, the physical senses, and even empathic abilities, and were implied to possess telepathic abilities as well, which could be used to attack others. The Douwd could destroy on an epic scale, but they could not bring people back to life, only make such illusory recreations of them. They appeared to be nearly omnipotent, godlike beings; but not omniscient.
One individual from the Douwd race was revealed to the Federation: a being who referred to itself as "Kevin Uxbridge" while in Human form. He claimed to have lived in the Milky Way Galaxy for "many thousands of years". While in Human form, he met a Human woman named Rishon. The two fell in love, and were married for fifty-three years. When an alien vessel attacked their home planet and killed Rishon, he committed genocide of the attacking vessel's entire species. (TNG: "The Survivors")
