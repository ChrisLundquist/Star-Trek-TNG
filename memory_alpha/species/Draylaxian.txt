Draylaxians were a species from the Alpha Quadrant. They were native to the planet Draylax. 
In the sweet spot aboard the NX-class starship Enterprise NX-01 in 2151, Commander Charles Tucker III asked helmsman Ensign Travis Mayweather, who had visited Draylax once aboard the ECS Horizon, if a certain rumor he had heard about Draylaxian women was true. Mayweather confirmed the rumor, indicating that Draylaxian women did indeed have "three" of something. He went on to claim to have had "firsthand, secondhand, and thirdhand" experiences with Draylaxian women. (ENT: "Broken Bow")
