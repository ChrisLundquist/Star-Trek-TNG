The Drella of the planet Alpha Carinae V was a lifeform that derived nourishment from the emotion of love.
In 2267, Spock asked the USS Enterprise's main computer whether Redjac, a non-corporeal predator that fed on fear, could exist, and whether there was a precedent for it in the Milky Way Galaxy. The computer mentioned the Drella as an example of a species that fed on an emotion. (TOS: "Wolf in the Fold")
