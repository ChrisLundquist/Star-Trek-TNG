The Drookmani were a spacefaring humanoid species. It was considered an act of war for a Federation starship to fire on a Drookmani ship.
In 2380, a ship of Drookmani scavengers discovered the wreckage of an Antares-type freighter and reported it to Starfleet. However, their captain was dissatisfied with the finder's reward they were offered by Captain Carol Freeman of the USS Cerritos, and instead claimed the salvage as their own, resulting in a hostile exchange. (LD: "Terminal Provocations")
In 2381, the Drookmani looted the wreckage of the starships lost at the Battle of Kalla, including the attempted capture of Peanut Hamper. Eventually, a Drookmani vessel salvaged Sam Rutherford's old oculus, leading to the captain falling under Badgey's control. (LD: "A Mathematically Perfect Redemption", "A Few Badgeys More")
