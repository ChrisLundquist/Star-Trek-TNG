Edosians (or Edoans) were a six-limbed, triped species native to the planet Edos.


== Biology ==
Edosians had three arms, three legs, and usually three fingers on each hand – also called "hand-toes" – although at least one individual had five digits. (Star Trek: The Animated Series; LD: "Room for Growth"; SNW: "Wedding Bell Blues") They were quite strong despite their relatively slender build: a single blow from an Edosian's fist was capable of breaking a desk in half. Additionally, they could emit a guttural roar when angered. (LD: "Much Ado About Boimler")
They were also highly flexible, capable of turning their necks a full 360 degrees, allowing them to observe their surroundings without turning their bodies.
They were susceptible to the Dramia II plague. (TAS: "Albatross") While some smoked, others occasionally wore respirators. (LD: "Much Ado About Boimler", "The Inner Fight")


== History ==

An Edosian Starfleet lieutenant named Arex was a member of the USS Enterprise crew from 2269 to 2270. (Star Trek: The Animated Series)
An Edosian medical specialist commanded the Division 14 ship Osler in 2380. (LD: "Much Ado About Boimler")
Another Edosian specialist, Toz, operated a retreat aboard the Dove in 2381. (LD: "Room for Growth")
In 2382, an Edosian officer was a member of the crew of the experimental ship USS Protostar. (PRO: "Last Flight of the Protostar, Part I")
