The Flaxians were a spacefaring humanoid species from the Alpha or Beta Quadrant. Flaxian assassins were known to use pheromonic sensors in their explosive devices.
Based on Retaya's appearance, Flaxians had mottled light brown skin with bumped ridges above the eyes, along the mouth and on the nose and nostrils. Their foreheads included a central line of silvery nodules. Tendrils extended from their foreheads and could be incorporated into the hair, while more tendrils hung from their chins. Flaxians also had tendrils that hung from the back of their hands.  (DS9: "Improbable Cause")
