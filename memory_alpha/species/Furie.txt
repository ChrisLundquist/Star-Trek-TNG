The Furies were a "species" of Human-alien hybrid humanoids. Physically, they were characterized by pale mottled skin, sunken black eyes, and protruding jaws, and they exhibited a peculiar optical effect that left after-images as they moved. Their spoken language consisted of growls and clicks.
In the 32nd century, the Furies were infamous for being vicious marauders and cannibals, who often killed their hostages even after their demands were met. According to Starfleet Intelligence, the Furies had skirmished with the Venari Ral in Sector 119 until they were driven away by Nus Braka using some type of specialized weapon.
In 3195, a band of Furies led by N'Duwo Skra attacked a team of Starfleet cadets on a training mission to the USS Miyazaki. Starfleet was forced to bargain with Braka for his knowledge on how to neutralize them. Braka stated that the Furies' sadism stemmed from their being in constant agony. He further claimed that sonic weapons were effective against them because they were part-lynar, meaning their inner ears were susceptible to high sonic frequencies. 
Ultimately, it was revealed that the Furies had actually been working with Braka all along, acting as diversions for a scheme to ransack Starbase J19-Alpha. The Furies who boarded the Miyazaki were killed by a telepathic attack from Tarima Sadal. (SA: "Come, Let's Away")
