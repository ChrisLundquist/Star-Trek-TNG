The Galadorans were a spacefaring species by the late 24th century. Their homeworld was Galador II.
In 2370, a ship allegedly being a Galador freighter left Deep Space 9 in 2370. However, the Galadorans didn't have any ships in the Bajor sector, so the ship was identified as belonging to the Maquis. (DS9: "The Maquis, Part I")
