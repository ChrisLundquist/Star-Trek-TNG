The Gallamites were a spacefaring humanoid species.


== Physiology ==
Gallamites had "toothy smiles," and transparent skulls through which pink brains twice the size of a Human's were visible. They had three digits on each hand (two fingers and a thumb). (DS9: "The Maquis, Part I"; LD: "The Best Exotic Nanite Hotel")


== History ==
Captain Boday, a Gallamite ship commander, was a frequent visitor of Deep Space 9 during the 2370s. (DS9: "The Maquis, Part I", et al.)
In 2375, during the Dominion War, the Gallamites contributed duranium to the Federation Alliance. (DS9: "Penumbra")
In 2382, two Gallamites were among the tourists visiting the Cosmic Duchess cruise ship. (LD: "The Best Exotic Nanite Hotel")
